About Me

Hi there! I'm Abimbola Olawunmi, a passionate and driven professional with a knack for turning innovative ideas into reality. Based in the vibrant city of Lagos, Nigeria, I thrive in dynamic environments where creativity meets technology.

With a BSc in Mass Communication, I have honed my skills as a full-stack web developer, blockchain developer, copywriter, social media manager, video editor, and graphic designer. My journey has been fueled by a relentless curiosity and a commitment to excellence.

I'm known for my versatility, creativity, and problem-solving abilities, which have been instrumental in delivering successful projects and client satisfaction. Over the past three years, I've had the privilege of working with House of Tara, Chevron Employee Members Cooperative Society (CEMCS), and various businesses on Instagram. I also played a key role in the start-up process and launch of an art school.

My dedication to excellence earned me the title of Staff of the Month during my stay at CEMCS, a successful launch of the art school, and letters of recommendation from my previous employers. I believe that the best solutions are crafted through collaboration and a deep understanding of user needs.

When I'm not managing diverse professional activities, you can find me playing the piano, singing, baking, listening to music, or reading, constantly seeking inspiration from the world around me.
